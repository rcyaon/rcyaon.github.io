We are Dozer

Dozer Incorporated is one of the many specialized companies stemming from Spartan Robotics Incorporated. Dozer is a diverse group of engineers, who have designed a robot, Rubble, capable of performing controlled, safe demolition. Rubble's goal is to effectively demolish and relocate rubble and debris without jeopardizing withstanding ecosystems and the surrounding populous. As a company devoted to the safety of the environment and community, we promise to adequately fulfill the tasks and responsibilities that accompany demolition.

Company Members

The members of our engineering team, headed by our CEO, are responsible for all aspects of the products created by Dozer. These team members are responsible for implementing the latest technologies and creating robotic solutions for a variety of issues and proposals presented to them.

Marketing

The members of our marketing team, headed by our CCO and COO, are responsible for the marketing and advertising of products, the building of company reputation, communication with other companies for growth, and holding meetings and creating presentations to further meet the needs of clients. All to influence potential clients to purchase Spartan Robotics Inc. products. Members in marketing include Lia Bridges, April Reep, Lena Conde-Araujo, Isaac Cavazos, River Dowdy, Mason Elliott, Lilian Garcia, and Kayloni Hartsfield.

Booth

The members of our exhibit team were responsible for creating a booth that puts the notion of safe demolition and our robot's capability of accomplishing it on display. This team is composed of individuals skilled in areas such as creativity, construction, storytelling, and art. Community Outreach

Homecoming Setup & Teardown

Throughout the year, we have volunteered locally, and even more so in the district. Our marketing and booth team helped organize Porter High Schools' annual Homecoming dance.

Kid Stem Activity Day

Hosted a STEM-related event involving the students in New Caney ISD. We accessed their problem-solving skills and taught them the overall importance of a design process and teamwork within STEM, by utilizing the concept of building with spaghetti noodles and marshmallows.

Set Building For Theatre Department

Aided with the overall creation and designing of the Fall plays set. Along with programming lights, adjusting barrels, and patchwork.

Trunk or Treat

Participated in a trunk or treat in The Heights on Valley Ranch Assisted Living providing candy to the senior residents there.
